Goldragon's Drawings- WoF Dragons

Structure of Dragon: Head

The head can be made many different ways, but I like to start with a square. Then, for the snout, I add a diagonal line about 1/4 away from the top of the square. Approximately 68 degrees would work, and the length depends on the age of your dragon. Then, make a horizontal line that starts at the bottom of the square and ends at the end of the diagonal line. Connect the lines with a vertical line. Finally, add horns and other features that are unique to that tribe. (Picture to the left is NOT my art!!!)

Structure of Dragon: Body

The body is made up of circles like remotely any other sketch skeleton, so the structure will be fairly simple. Keep in mind that a dragon is basically a snake with arms, legs, wings, horns, ect. Don't add any unnececary bulges because, well, it'll just ruin the whole thing. The body witout any of the detials is made up of 2 circles parallel to each other. You should probally place the second one a bit higher than then the first one. Connect these circles with two lines, one on top and one on the bottom. Make them nice and curvy. Connect the neck with the first circle
